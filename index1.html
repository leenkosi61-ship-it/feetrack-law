Data Mode
The tool opens with demo data so you can explore every feature. When ready for real use, clear everything and start with your actual firm data. You can always reload demo data.
After clearing — how to add your real data:
1. Firm Setup — enter your real firm name, logo, bank details
2. Staff & Rates — add your real team and their rates
3. Clients & Matters — add your active clients and matters
4. Live Timer — start logging. Or use Manual Entry to add past work.
